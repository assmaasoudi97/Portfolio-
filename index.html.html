Case studies

Strategic Initiatives

Each case demonstrates the same pattern: diagnose the structural failure, redesign the system, and build accountability into delivery.

Case Study 01

Restoring Accountability in Home Medical Service Delivery

Marsal Foundation

Challenge

100 registered patient cases with no audit trail, no verification model, and institutional resources deployed without accountability.

Actions

Introduced GPS-based arrival and departure verification, built a 24-hour patient follow-up protocol, designed complete case documentation, expanded the contractor network, appointed specialist consultants, and instituted daily and monthly reporting to management and the CEO.

90%+

Service completion rate achieved, with a full audit trail and complete operational and financial visibility established for the first time.

Case Study 02

25,000 Cases — Organizational Data Recovery

Marsal Foundation

Challenge

25,000 patient cases trapped in a system without analysis capability while departments failed to update records and leadership had no operational visibility.

Actions

Analyzed all 25,000 cases over 8 months, classified each by status, worked directly with departments to recover context, and produced a CEO-level report with specific actions.

Tools

Google SheetsOdooInternal Cases System
25,000

Cases made visible and actionable, stalled files reactivated, and underperforming departments identified and corrected.

Case Study 03

Building a Special Needs Education Operating System

Fikra Educational Center

Challenge

Four simultaneous operational failures: no community integration model, no effective room design, no individual session access structure, and no documentation system.

Actions

Negotiated partnerships and delivered 20+ integration days, redesigned session rooms to reduce distraction, built a joker-specialist staffing model, and created reusable session documentation templates.

Long-Term Impact

Documentation frameworks and operational improvements that continued supporting child outcomes and delivery continuity long after implementation.

Case Study 04

Enterprise Software Client Delivery Operations

15+ clients across Egypt and GCC

Challenge

Simultaneous delivery across Android, iOS, web, and dashboards, with client resistance, fragmented communication habits, and inconsistent delivery workflows.

Actions

Owned the full client lifecycle from contract to delivery, migrated 15 clients from WhatsApp to a ticketing system in 6 weeks, managed sprint planning and backlogs, and supported early KMM framework adoption.

Tools

JiraTrelloTicketing SystemsSprint Planning
6 weeks

To full system adoption, with consistent delivery across 15+ simultaneous clients and stronger client relationships.
